A private intelligence dossier page for high-ticket outreach to Dubai billionaires and sovereign wealth funds. This page presents Kings Driven as an exclusive strategic partner — not a vendor. It contains a curated target intelligence feed, a high-ticket pitch section, and a private enquiry form.

DESIGN SYSTEM (REQUIRED):
- Platform: Web, Desktop-first
- Theme: Dark, sovereign luxury, billionaire-quiet
- Background: Sovereign Black (#0a0a0a) for page, Obsidian Surface (#121212) for cards
- Primary Accent: Emperor's Gold (#f59e0b) for CTAs and key highlights
- Text Primary: Pure White (#ffffff) for headlines
- Text Secondary: Zinc-400 (#a1a1aa) for body copy
- Borders: White/5 opacity on cards, brightening to amber-500/30 on hover
- Buttons: Rounded-xl (12px), amber-500 primary / zinc-900 secondary
- Cards: Rounded-3xl (24px), dark surface, barely-there borders
- Typography: Extreme weight contrast — bold heavy headlines, lighter body
- Spacing: Generous — py-32 to py-40 between sections
- Mood: Exclusive, authoritative, mission-critical

Page Structure:
1. **Navigation Bar:** Fixed top, Kings Driven logo left, minimal links right, "Request Briefing" amber pill CTA
2. **Hero Section:** Large headline "INTELLIGENCE. NOT PROPOSALS." with subtext about selective partnerships. Small amber badge: "CLEARED FOR VIEWING". No images — pure typography dominance.
3. **Target Intelligence Feed:** 3 cards showing anonymized intelligence on target entities (e.g. "Sovereign Fund Alpha — $47B AUM — AI Infrastructure Focus"). Each card has a classification label (ALPHA / GAMMA / CLASSIFIED), entity type, investment focus, and a "View Pitch" button.
4. **Pitch Section:** Full-width dark card with "THE KINGS DRIVEN PROTOCOL" headline. 3 numbered steps: "01. IDENTIFY" / "02. INFILTRATE" / "03. DOMINATE". Amber number labels, white step titles, zinc body text.
5. **Private Enquiry Form:** Minimal dark form — Name, Fund/Entity, Brief, "Submit for Review" amber button. Note: "Applications reviewed within 48h. We decline 9 of 10."
6. **Footer:** Minimal — Kings Driven wordmark, Dubai UAE, © 2026
